Try out different study methods and time management techniques to help you study and focus more effectively.

Do you know about the pomodoro technique? If not, the pomodoro technique is a time management technique developed by Francesco Cirillo which uses splits 2 hours into 25-minute time slots of focused work and 5 minute break intervals.





Do you find to-do lists more effective? Make and manage your short-term tasks and keep track of all your deadlines.









Do you want to create a timetable to plan more effectively? Schedule all of your short-term and long-term tasks on a chart.
