How to use the Pomodoro Timer:

1. Start the 25 minute timer and focus on your task
2. Stop focusing after the 25 minute timer finishes & Start the 5 minute break timer
3. Repeat steps 1 & 2 for 4 intervals
4. Take a 15 minute break and start the 15 minute timer
5. Repeat all steps until you are satisfied with all the work you have done :)
